interesting facts about origami

  • Origami is dervived from two japanese words "Ori" which means folded and "kami" which means paper.
  • Previously it was called "Orikata" which means folded shapes.
  • initially the art of paper folding was restricted to only ceremonial occasions because paper was scarce and expesive
  • There are 80 different types of origami
